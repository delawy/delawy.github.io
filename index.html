Some of our Work

Predicted Time till Arrival
Vehicle Arrival Prediction

Our Vehicle arrival prediction software uses readily available GPS technology and proprietary algorithm, which uses vehicle movement time series data, mathematical model of complex traffic flow on highly accurate geospatial networks, to track vehicles and predict vehicle arrival time. The accurate predictions and the customizable algorithm which drives this software delivers many advantages regarding logistics and travel, including efficient fleet management, accurate prediction of delivery time, travel schedule management, school bus monitoring and accurate arrival time.

Crime Prediction
Crime Prediction

Delawy’s powerful crime prediction software enables law enforcement agencies to predict criminal activities with an accuracy rate of 85.3%. Our customizable predictive analytics identifies patterns that may repeat into the future. We primarily examination past crimes, but also analyse numerous factors such as weather, location of school, transportation hubs, pubic events to precisely predict the locations, times, and types of crimes that are most likely to occur. The objective of these methods is to develop effective strategies that will prevent crime or make investigation efforts more effective.

Human Emotion Prediction
Human Emotion Prediction

Our voice emotion recognition software can detect the mood of people by analysing their voice. The core model is trained to observe the phonatory-articulatory level and the acoustic level to classify people’s voices based on emotions. The voice emotion recognition is a power technology which can be used in many service oriented businesses, such as monitoring customer service centers, improving the efficiency of telemarketing.
